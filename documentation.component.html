Introduction

Art Institute of Chicago project - an online platform that brings all the artworks present in the prestigious museum. This project aims to allow art lovers from around the world to explore and admire the incredible collection of the Art Institute of Chicago from the comfort of their own homes.

The website contains a vast collection of artworks from a wide range of genres and styles, including paintings, sculptures, photographs and many more. Easy browsing and searching for specific artworks by entering keywords or phrases into the search bar, which will filter and display matching results. One can add your favorite artworks to their wishlist, which allows easy tracking of favorite pieces and discover new art that fits within unique interests.

Our website offers detailed information and high-quality images of each artwork. Our user-friendly interface allows you to view the artworks in great detail and appreciate their aesthetic beauty up close. You can learn about the history and context of each artwork, and delve into the inspiring stories behind their creation.

Navigation

The website's navigation structure is designed to be simple and intuitive, allowing users to easily find their way around the site and quickly access the content they are interested in. The primary navigation element in our website is a navbar located at the top of each page and contains the following buttons:

  • Home: Allows users to quickly navigate back to the homepage of the website from any other page.
  • Search: This button allows users to access our powerful search functionality, where they can enter keywords or phrases to find specific artworks or information.
  • Wishlist: This button leads to the wishlist page, where users can see all the artworks they have saved to their wishlist.
  • Documentation: This button leads to the documentation page where users can read and learn more about using the website, including detailed descriptions about our key features, navigation, and other important information.

Each of these buttons is linked to a specific page on the website where users can access the content and features that are related to the button clicked. Clicking on any one of them will take the user directly to that page, providing a seamless and user-friendly experience. Moreover, we have kept the navbar consistent throughout the website, allowing users to navigate the site confidently and with ease regardless of which page they are on.
